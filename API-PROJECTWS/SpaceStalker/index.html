Space Stalker

Space Stalker is a real-time space tracking app that lets you peek into humanity’s greatest adventure — life in orbit. Powered by the Open Notify API, it instantly shows you how many astronauts are currently in space and who they are. With a clean, glassmorphism-inspired design, the app makes this fascinating data feel alive and accessible, turning raw numbers into a cosmic story unfolding above us. Space Stalker bridges everyday curiosity with real-time space exploration, letting you feel connected to the astronauts orbiting Earth right now.
