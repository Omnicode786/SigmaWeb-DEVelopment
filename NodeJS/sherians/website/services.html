SERVICES

From design to doorstep, we manage every stage of your apparel production with precision, transparency,
and care — so you can focus on growing your brand.

We handle the complexities of apparel manufacturing so you don’t have to. With trusted partners across Asia & dedicated
teams on the ground, we ensure your vision is executed with consistency, quality, and full transparency.

From initial product development to factory matchmaking, order management, quality assurance, and global delivery

Product Development

Bringing Your Vision to Life

  • • Tech Pack Creation – Precise technical documents with specs, construction details, and measurements.
  • • Sample Development – Prototypes made to test fit, form, and finish before bulk production.
  • • Trend Forecasting – Stay ahead with insights into upcoming styles, colors, and materials.
  • • Fabric & Trim Sourcing – Access to a wide library of mills and trim vendors suited to your needs.

Factory Sourcing

Access Trusted Manufacturing Partners

  • • Flexible MOQs – Catering to both startups and established brands.
  • • Product-Type Expertise – Factories matched by strength in specific garments or techniques.
  • • Certified Factories – Factories in Pakistan, China & Bangladesh, thoroughly audited and proven.
  • • Ethical & Sustainable Production – With a focus on responsible practices and transparency..

Merchandising

Smooth Operations, Every Step of the Way

  • • Approval Management – Trim cards, fabric headers, and pre-production samples tracked.
  • • Weekly Progress Reports – Visual status updates and milestone tracking.
  • • Accurate Costing & Lead Times – Transparent budgeting and delivery planning.
  • • Daily Vendor Coordination – Active follow-ups with suppliers, factories, and printers.

Quality Control

No Compromise on Quality

  • • Detailed QC Reports – Photo-based reports for full visibility.
  • • Lab Testing Coordination – For durability, shrinkage, colorfastness, and more.
  • • Inline & Final AQL Inspections – In-process and post-production checks.
  • • Defect Tagging & Resolution – Issues logged with corrective actions.

Logistics & Shipping

Hassle-Free Global Delivery

  • • Doorstep Delivery – Global shipping directly to your destination.
